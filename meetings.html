The club meets every Thursday at 6pm at Brewsterâ€™s Pub in Cary, NC. The main event of our weekly meetings is a tournament. Tournaments consist of seven-point matches in the Championship Bracket and five-point matches in the Consolation Bracket. Players are guaranteed at least two matches. All matches use the doubling cube. There is no entry fee for the tournament, but there is a completely optional side pool.
