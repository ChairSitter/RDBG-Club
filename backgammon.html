Backgammon is one of the oldest and most popular board games in the world. It combines exciting dice rolls, deep strategic planning, and an elegent betting system into one beastly mind-sport. Roll the dice to move, block, and hit, and develop a plan to finish the race before your opponent. Analyze the board and use the doubling cube to challenge your opponent with a well-timed wager. Come out to the club to learn more and hone your skills with us!
